# Contributing to BetaTesterArena

We welcome contributions from the community!

## How to Help

- **Report bugs** — Open an issue with steps to reproduce
- **Suggest features** — Check our existing feature requests and add a 👍 or open a new issue
- **Spread the word** — Share https://betatesterarena.com with indie devs who need beta testing
- **Become a tester** — Play games and leave quality feedback on the platform
